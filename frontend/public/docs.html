Core Features

What Makes Us Different

Live Mandi Grounding

Every price is verified via real-time web search. No outdated training data - only today's actual market rates from AgMarknet and e-NAM.

Neural Memory

The AI remembers your name, past searches, and current screen context. Ask "What did I search yesterday?" - it knows!

District Accuracy

Tap anywhere on India's map. OpenStreetMap reverse geocoding identifies the exact district for location-specific recommendations.

Price Forecasting

Beautiful area charts showing 4 months historical + TODAY + 4 months predicted prices with risk/bearish bounds.

Zero Downtime

Automatic API key rotation between 3 Gemini keys. If one exhausts quota, the system switches instantly - no errors for users.

Persistent Storage

SQLite database saves all users, chat history, and reports. Nothing is lost - even after closing the app.

User Journey

How It Works

1

Login with Phone

Enter your phone number and name. The system creates a personalized account or restores your existing data.

2

Tap on the Map

Click anywhere on India. The system detects your district via GPS coordinates and prepares a location-specific analysis.

3

AI Generates Recommendations

Gemini 2.5 Flash analyzes soil data, market trends, and regional patterns to suggest 5-10 profitable crops with current prices.

4

Get Full Plan

Click any crop for detailed strategy: live price charts, cultivation techniques, fertilizer schedules, and government schemes.

5

Chat with AI

Ask follow-up questions in the Chat tab. The AI knows your history and can recall past searches or explain current recommendations.
